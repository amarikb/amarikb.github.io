What is Kpop?

"
(abbreviation of korean pop; Korean: 케이팝)
is a genre of popular music originating in South Korea.
The term Korean popular music describes a modern form of South Korean pop that is influenced by styles and genres from around the world, such as experimental, rock, jazz, gospel, hip hop, R&B, reggae, electronic dance, folk, country, and classical on top of its traditional Korean music roots.
